Instructions for viewing computer localhost on your ipad

  1. On your machine, get localhost started up. Note the port number (a four digit number after localhost)
  2. Connect your ipad and computer to the same wifi network
  3. Get your machines ip address using 'ifconfig' in your terminal
  4. In ipad safari, type your ip address followed by a colon, then the port number. For example; if your IP address is 123.42.32.13 and the port is 3000. Typing 123.42.32.13:3000 will load up the website that's running on your machines local host
